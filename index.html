About the Game

Five Nights at Derpos (FNaD) is a fan-made horror game inspired by Five Nights at Freddy’s. Created entirely in Scratch, it combines classic survival mechanics with unique characters and a creepy atmosphere, subtly tying into the wider FNaF universe.

As the night guard at various Derpo's locations, you must survive five increasingly intense nights by monitoring cameras, managing resources, and avoiding animatronic nightmares.

FNaD adds its own twist on classic gameplay, from off-brand camera systems to animatronics with specific movement patterns, ensuring every night is a challenge!

About the Programming

Built entirely in Scratch, FNaD uses visual programming with drag-and-drop logic blocks to bring the game to life. Despite the simplicity, it allows for complex systems like camera switching, power management, and animatronic AI.

  1. Design the sprites – Starting with placeholders, I refine them in my own unique style.
  2. Program the interactions – I craft the logic that makes the game work, which is the most fun part!
  3. Add sounds – I borrow a few from FNaF (sorry, Scott!), but also make my own when possible.
  4. Test and refine gameplay – It’s all about finding that balance between fun, challenging, and fair gameplay.
