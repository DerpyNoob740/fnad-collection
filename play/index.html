Five Nights at Derpo's 1: Remastered

Story

You are Frank Fritz, and you have no money. You need a job, so you find a listing for a local pizza place. The pay is good, and the work seems easy—just watch the place overnight. Sounds simple. But as the nights pass, you realize things aren’t what they said they would be.

Five Nights at Derpo's 2: Remastered

Story

You are Samuel Martin, having just graduated from high school. In search of employment, you apply at the local arcade you frequented as a child. The interview goes smoothly, and you land the position of night guard. That was simple enough. However, as the nights drag on, you discover the job is not what they claimed it would be.
